Frequently Asked Questions

What types of services does WellIntern provide?

We're an all-in-one place for hiring interns across the globe. Our tools include sourcing, job listings, employer branding, a free applicant management system, and AI-powered sourcing.

How does WellIntern differ from other job boards?

WellIntern specializes in connecting companies with top intern talent. We offer a curated pool of candidates, advanced AI-powered matching, and tools specifically designed for internship programs.

Is WellIntern free to use?

We offer both free and paid plans. You can post jobs and access basic features for free. Our premium plans offer advanced features like AI-powered candidate matching, unlimited job postings, and priority support.

How can I improve my company's visibility on WellIntern?

Create a compelling company profile, regularly post job openings, and engage with candidates through our platform. Our employer branding tools can help showcase your company culture and attract top talent.

What kind of support does WellIntern offer?

We offer email and chat support for all users. Premium plan subscribers also get access to dedicated account managers and priority support for faster response times.
